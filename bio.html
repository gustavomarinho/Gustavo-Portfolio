Hi, I'm Gustavo!

I'm currently a Product Design Manager at Inklocker,
a Print-on-Demand company in Los Angeles, CA.
I manage an small team and all of the work related to our website, product, and brand.

What I love most about being a designer is the way I connect people, create stories, and inspire emotions.

I'm not just looking to create a beautiful face for the product. It's much more than making things look good, it's about understanding the user, how they think, their limitations, and their goals. My passion lies in creating great products and services.

I grew up immersed in the arts, sports (mostly football) and culture surfing. When I'm not at work, I spend most of my time exploring new places, trying new wines and traveling.
